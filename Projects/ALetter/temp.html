Make Hearts Closer

Time passed slowly in the past. Carriage, horse, mail are all slow. Even a greeting needs needs to wait for many days.
Now time is passing fast. It's too fast that there is no time to say good morning and no time for hug.

In fact, time is innocent.

No matter how slow the past time was or how fast it is in the current, treasure the earth under your feet and the love around your heart with your best, no matter in the past or in the right moment.
